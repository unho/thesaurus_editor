Thesaurus-editor allows you to import words, stablish relationships between words, and export this data.

Word relationships

There are several available relationship types:

  • Synonym
  • Antonym
  • Related: for stablishing another kind of relationship.

For each kind of relationship type, a given word can have several different relationships or meanings, which in fact correspond to different concepts.

Getting words

You can get words to relate from two sources:

  • Searching for a word by using the «Search» form.
  • Go to the «Suggestions» tab for getting machine-suggested (based on word similarity) or user-suggested words.

Click in any list item for marking it as active and use any of the the buttons or keyboard shortcuts for performing an action.

Keyboard shortcuts

For quicker usage you can perform actions by using your keyboard.

Keys What they do
A Adds the active word to the antonyms list.
S Adds the active word to the synonyms list.
R Adds the active word to the related words list.
Shift + A Adds the active word to the antonyms list as a new meaning.
Shift + S Adds the active word to the synonyms list as a new meaning.
Shift + R Adds the active word to the related words list as a new meaning.
H Shows this help dialog.
F Put the focus on the search input.
I Go to the import page.
E Export the current word list.
Enter Triggers the search and makes active the first word in the search results.
Down Puts the focus on the next word in the search results.
Down Puts the focus on the previous word in the search results.
Right Goes to the next word page.
Left Goes to the previous word page.
